Canary

It is considered one of the most beautiful types of birds, as some consider it a decorative bird, and many work to raise it at home, as canaries are covered in yellow with brown spots, and the original home of canaries is the Canary Islands, and canaries are also distinguished by their names of many birds that fall under them.

Toucan

This type of bird is relatively large in size compared to the types of sparrows. It also has a large beak and is characterized by its short wings. It also has a thick neck. It is one of the birds that live in the thickets and feed on some types of insects, some grains and types of fruit.

Henna
